# Group AI News Brief

Convert raw AI news into concise updates for group chats.

## Format

- 3-line speed summary
- 5 key headlines with one-sentence impact each
- Optional section by lens: product / investment / policy
- Closing: one actionable suggestion for builders

## Rules

- Prefer same-day or last-24h items.
- Label uncertainty clearly.
- Avoid hype words unless supported by sources.
- Keep each bullet under 40 Chinese characters when possible.

## Output variants

- `社群转发版` (short)
- `深度解读版` (long)
- `投资判断版` (bullish/neutral/bearish tags)
